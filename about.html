Zachary Viet Pine presents:

Siddhartha

Discover an innovative performance, an hour and a half in the making, where local dancers and singers unite to bring to life a concept by Zachary Pine. Inspired by Herman Hesse's novel "Siddhartha," this unique production is a work in progress. Join us for one of our special showings this weekend and become a part of our creative journey. Your presence and feedback are invaluable to us. Don't miss this exciting experience!

Hosted by the First Unitarian Universalist Church of New Orleans
